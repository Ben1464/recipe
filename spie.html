Ready Boche de noel

Boche de noel is a French name for a christimas cakes shaped like a log. This is a heavenly flourless chocolate cake rolled with chocolate whipped cream. Traditionally, Buche de noel is decorated with confectioners sugar resemble snow on a yule log.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • Half cup confectioner's sugar
  • Half cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 Teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 6 egg yolk
  • Half cup white sugar
  • Half cup sweeteened cocoa powder
  • 1 Teaspoon table salt
  • Confectioners sugar for dusting

Directions

  • Step 1

Preheat oven to 375 degrees F(190 degree C). line a 10*15 inch jellyroll pan with parchment paper.in a large bowl, whip cream,1/2 cup confectioners sugar,1/2 cup cocoa, and 1 teaspoon vanilla untill thick stiff. Refrigerate

  • Step 2

In large bowl, use an electric mixer to beat yolk with 1/2 cup sugar until thick and pale.Blend in 1/3cup cocoa, 1,1/2 teaspoons vanilla, and sal. in large glass bowl, using clean beataers, whip eggs whites to soft peaks. Gradually add 1/4 cup sugar, and beat until white stiff peaks. immediately fold the yolk mixture into the whites. spread the better evenely into the prepared pan.

  • Step 3

    • Bake for 12 to 15 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the cake springs back when lightly touched. Dust a clean dishtowel with confectioners sugar.Run a knife around the edge of the pan, and turn the warm cake out of the towel. Remove the discard parchment paper. starting at the edge of the cake,roll the cake up with the towel. cool for 30 min.

  • Step 4

    • Unroll the cake, and spread the filling to within 1 inch of the edge. Roll the cake up with the filling inside. Place seam side down onto a serving plate, and refrigerate until serving. Dust with confectioners' sugar before serving.
